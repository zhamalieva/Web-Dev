Recipe #1

  • Time: 25 minute
  • Serves: in large flat bowls.
Ingredients Quantity

Chicken fillet 80 g
fettuccine pasta 60 g
Champignons 2 pieces
Garlic 1 clove
Cheese 10 g
Cream 25% 100 ml
Parsley taste

Step 1: Boil a nest of pasta in lightly salted water.

Step 2: Chicken fillet, cut into small pieces, fry until half cooked. Before frying, pepper the chicken, salt, add a pinch of curry and leave for 15 minutes - this way it becomes juicier.

Step 3: Add sliced mushrooms and fry until tender in vegetable oil.

Step 4: Add finely chopped garlic.

Step 5: Pour in the cream (it is necessary to warm it up a little first so that it does not curdle), bring to a boil and turn it off.

Step 5: Serve the pasta, pouring the sauce over the top and sprinkle with the cheese.
